Video Games

A World Renowned Hobby

Introduction

Video games are one of the most popular forms of entertainment in the world. Using proprietary hardware and software, developed by different companies, you can interactively explore various worlds and ideas that have been put onto a screen. The most common theme between all games is that there is some form of interactivity involved, but the genres and experiences between individual games can vary depending on personal experience.

Instructions

  1. Considered by many to be the first video game ever created, Spacewar! was created in 1961 at MIT.
  2. Although there have been many hardware manufacturers over the course of video game history, as of 2020, there are five major commercial video game hardware manufacturers: Nintendo, Microsoft, Sony, Personal Computer and Mobile Phone devices.
  3. Although now synonymous with video games, Nintendo actually started as a playing card company in 1889, well before consumer electronics were readily available.
